# Call log — LA Rent Desk validation

Every conversation gets a row, logged within 10 minutes of hanging up. A call with no row didn't happen.

**A conversation counts toward the gates only if** it ran 10+ minutes AND the discovery questions actually got answered (a voicemail, a "sounds cool" hallway chat, or a text exchange doesn't count).

## Scoreboard (update after every call)

| Metric | Count | Gate |
|---|---|---|
| Qualified conversations (landlord) | 0 | 50 = decision point |
| Qualified conversations (PM firm) | 0 | 10 = decision point |

**Kill gate:** 50 qualified landlord conversations + 10 PM conversations with < 5 cards and 0 pilots → park the idea and write up why.

## Weekly review (Sundays, 15 min)

1. Cards this week vs. last — trending toward 25 or flat?
2. Which discovery question produced the strongest reactions? Lead with it next week.
3. Top objection this week → does the answer bank rebuttal actually work live? Rewrite if not.
4. Any question Tanner couldn't answer → add it to the answer bank with a verified, dated answer.
5. Referral list: who hasn't been called yet?
