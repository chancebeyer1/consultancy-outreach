# ICP — Leasing/rental agents in LA with many small-landlord relationships

Who we're targeting: **rental-side agents in Los Angeles County whose business runs on
relationships with lots of private landlords** — the people who fill units for dozens
of mom-and-pop owners. Each one is a potential referral partner: their landlords trust
them, and rent-increase questions land on them constantly even though it's not their
job. Fit = "does this person personally work with many small LA landlords?"

## Segments (score the fit, label which one)

- **independent_leasing** — Independent leasing agents / rental specialists at small
  brokerages who rep many private owners' units across LA. The classic "I have 40
  landlords in my phone" profile. Highest fit.
- **rental_realtor** — Licensed agents whose profile centers on rentals/leasing (not
  sales), working with private landlords. High fit.
- **boutique_leasing_mgr** — Leasing managers at small local PM/brokerage shops who
  personally own the landlord relationships. Medium-high fit.
- **locator** — Apartment locators/rental matchmakers with broad small-owner networks.
  Medium fit — tenant-paid models know fewer owners personally.

## Strong-fit signals (raise the score)

- Language like "representing landlords", "helping owners lease", unit counts,
  "serving [LA neighborhoods]" on the rental side
- Small-brokerage or solo branding (their name IS the business)
- Active about LA rentals: pricing posts, vacancy takes, tenant-screening content
- Long tenure in LA leasing (deep owner books)

## Disqualifiers (score → 0)

- On-site leasing consultants at institutional communities (Greystar-type lease-up
  teams) — one corporate owner, zero private-landlord relationships
- New-development lease-up specialists; commercial/retail leasing brokers
- Pure sales agents with no rental practice (and LA sales agents are Tanner's
  competitors — not this campaign's audience)
- Self-managing landlords (lr-landlords) and PM-firm staff (lr-property-managers)
- Vendors selling to agents; coaches; lead-gen sellers
- Outside LA County; outside the US
