# Voice — research-led, hand-typed (real-estate flavor)

These illustrate *how I write* — plain, lowercase-casual, a little uneven, no em-dashes,
contractions, one soft ask. The draft should match the active Offer's domain, not copy
these word for word.

## Connection note (cold, ≤300 chars)

> Hi {{first_name}}, running a team means the leads are yours to buy and yours to lose,
> every slow reply is your ad spend dying. researching exactly that gap for real estate
> teams and would genuinely value learning how you handle it.

> {{first_name}}, came across your team, building a named brand with your own lead flow
> is exactly the kind of operation I'm trying to learn from. not selling anything, just
> researching how teams actually run lead follow-up.

## First DM (post-accept, ≤500 chars)

> thanks for the connect. quick context since I doubt you remember the note, I build AI
> systems and I'm digging into how teams handle inbound leads: the 9pm zillow ping, sign
> calls during showings, the old database nobody re-touches. not pitching anything,
> genuinely trying to learn where leads actually fall through. could I borrow 15 min to
> hear how your team runs it? happy to share what I find across everyone I talk to.
