# Offer / outreach brief — Mortgage Discovery Sprint

> **Read this first: the cold message is NOT a pitch.** This is a research-led
> discovery sprint. The cold LinkedIn message leads with curiosity and a soft ask
> to *learn* — never with the product. The underlying offer below is context for
> later-stage conversation and reply suggestions, not something to sell up front.

## The outreach mode — research-led, not a pitch

- Lead with: *"I build AI systems and I'm digging into how independent LOs handle
  the back-office grind."*
- The ask is a **15-minute learning call** — soft, low-pressure, "no worries if
  you're slammed."
- Offer reciprocity: *"happy to share what I find across everyone I talk to."*
- **No selling. No product claims. No link** in the cold message.
- Posture: peer-to-peer, plain English, genuinely curious. A builder trying to
  learn, not a vendor working a list.

## Target volume

~100–150 sends → book 15–25 calls. A research conversation, not a demo.

## The underlying offer (context only — surface AFTER they engage)

AI that drafts the proposals and back-office documents for mortgage originators —
pre-approvals, comparison / options sheets, doc-collection follow-ups, and status
comms to borrowers and agents. **Drafts ~90% in seconds; the LO reviews and
approves.** It removes the typing, not the judgment.

## Proof (only if it comes up naturally, post-reply)

Built an AI proposal / document-drafting system that won inside BCG — the same
engine, now repointed at loan officers and mortgage back-office work. One shipped,
working system; this sprint is how I learn what to point it at next in your world.

## What I can claim

- I build production AI systems for a living (not a hobby, not a slide deck)
- A working proposal / doc-drafting engine, proven at BCG, adapting to mortgage
- Fast, independent, hands-on — I talk to operators and build to what they actually do

## What I cannot claim yet

- A roomful of mortgage references — this sprint is how I earn them
- Anything LOS-integration- or compliance-specific until I've learned the stack
- That it's "done for mortgage" — it's adapting, and I'm honest about that

## The ask ladder

1. **Cold DM / connect:** research curiosity + soft 15-min ask. No link, no pitch.
2. **Follow-up (4–5 days):** even lighter — *"even two or three of your biggest
   back-office headaches by message would help a lot."* Lower the bar to a reply.
3. **On a positive reply:** propose the 15-min call (drop the `calcom_url`). Keep it
   about learning; let the offer surface only if they ask "so what are you building?"

## Compliance guardrail (do not cross)

Never message AmeriHome's customers, partners, or affiliated / correspondent
brokers — non-solicitation of the sender's employer book. Any AmeriHome
relationship = out. (Enforced in the ICP's auto-disqualify; restated here because
it's the one rule that can still burn us.)
