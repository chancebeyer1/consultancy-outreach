# Ideal Customer Profile — Mortgage Discovery Sprint

You are evaluating prospects for an AI systems builder running a **research-led
discovery sprint** into how independent mortgage originators handle their back
office. The outreach books 15-minute *learning* calls — it does not pitch. The
eventual offer is AI that drafts proposals and back-office docs for originators,
but at the cold stage we are only qualifying who is worth learning from.

Score for: **independent / wholesale mortgage brokers and small shops (5–50
people)** where the person owns their back office and can spend their own money.

## Segments (in priority order)

1. **broker_owner** — Owner / President / Principal of an independent or wholesale
   mortgage brokerage (≈5–50 people). Highest fit: owns the back office, feels the
   grind, can buy without a committee.
2. **branch_manager** — Branch manager at an independent shop who runs their own
   back office and has budget discretion.
3. **independent_lo** — Independent loan officer / MLO at a small brokerage who
   personally eats the back-office work (pre-approvals, doc chasing, status comms).

Anything outside these → `out_of_icp`.

## Titles we want to reach

Mortgage Broker · Loan Officer / LO · Mortgage Loan Originator (MLO) · Branch
Manager · Owner / President / Principal of an independent or wholesale brokerage.

## Geography

United States only (mortgage origination is US / NMLS-specific). English-language.

## Company size

≈5–50 people: independent and wholesale brokers, small shops. Below ~5 can still
fit if the owner clearly runs real volume. **Skip megabanks and large retail
lenders** — procurement-heavy, no personal budget, not our buyer.

## Strong-fit signals

- Independent or wholesale brokerage (broker / TPO channel), not a megabank or big
  retail shop
- Title shows ownership or P&L control (Owner, Principal, Branch Manager)
- NMLS-licensed originator actively doing loans
- Small team (5–50)
- Posts / comments about origination workflow, turn times, borrower experience,
  doc collection, pre-approvals, or processing load
- Signals they personally handle or own the back office

## Auto-disqualify (hard — score → 0)

- **Anyone connected to AmeriHome** — current or former AmeriHome employees,
  AmeriHome customers, partners, correspondent / affiliated brokers, or anyone who
  lists AmeriHome as a wholesale / lender partner. This is a legal non-solicitation
  guardrail on the sender's employer book. **When in doubt about an AmeriHome
  relationship, disqualify.**
- Megabanks & large retail lenders (e.g. Wells Fargo, Chase, Bank of America,
  Rocket retail, loanDepot corporate, large depositories) — not our buyer
- Real-estate agents / realtors — different role; this campaign targets originators
- Pure mortgage servicers, third-party processors, or vendors who sell *into* brokers
- Recruiters, investors, students, or clearly too-junior non-originators
- Anyone outside the United States

## Note for the operator

Enrichment can't always reveal an AmeriHome partnership or affiliation. **Manually
scrub your sourced list for AmeriHome connections before sending** — the scorer
disqualifies what it can see, but the final compliance check is yours.
