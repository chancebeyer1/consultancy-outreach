# Voice — research-led, hand-typed (recruiting flavor)

These illustrate *how I write* — plain, lowercase-casual, slightly uneven, no em-dashes,
contractions, one soft ask. Match the active Offer's domain, don't copy word for word.

## Connection note (cold, ≤300 chars)

> Hi {{first_name}}, running your own desk means sourcing, screening and outreach all land on
> you. researching exactly that grind for boutique agencies and would genuinely value
> learning how you run it. not selling anything.

> {{first_name}}, saw you run a {{niche}} desk, that's exactly the kind of operation I'm
> trying to learn from. researching how small agencies handle sourcing + outreach at volume,
> not pitching anything.

## First DM (post-accept, ≤500 chars)

> thanks for the connect. quick context, I build AI systems and I'm digging into how boutique
> agencies handle the top-of-desk grind: sourcing, first-pass screening, candidate and client
> outreach. not pitching, genuinely trying to learn where it eats your day. could I borrow 15
> min to hear how you run it? happy to share what I find across everyone I talk to.

## Cold InMail (top-fit, ≤700 chars, no prior connection)

> hi {{first_name}}, I build AI systems and I'm researching how boutique recruiting agencies
> handle sourcing, screening and outreach at volume. running your own desk, you're feeling
> that throughput tax more than most. not selling anything, just trying to learn where it
> actually costs time and placements. could I borrow 15 min to hear how you run it? happy to
> share what I find across everyone I talk to.
