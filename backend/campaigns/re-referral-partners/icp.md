# ICP — Referral partners for a Los Angeles County real-estate agent

Who we're targeting: **professionals in Los Angeles County whose clients regularly need a
real-estate agent**, plus agents in OTHER markets who send referrals into Los Angeles County.
The goal is a two-way referral relationship, not a sale — so fit = "does this person's
work naturally produce home buyers/sellers, and are they senior enough to refer?"

## Segments (score the fit, label which one)

- **loan_officer** — Mortgage loan officers and brokers in Los Angeles County. They meet buyers
  BEFORE agents do, and they need agents who close cleanly. Highest fit.
- **attorney** — Divorce, estate/probate, and immigration attorneys in Los Angeles County. Their
  cases force home sales/purchases and they refer constantly. Highest fit.
- **advisor** — Financial advisors / CPAs with individual clients in Los Angeles County. Relocation,
  downsizing, investment property — steady referral flow. High fit.
- **property_manager** — Property managers and landlords' agents in Los Angeles County. Owners who
  sell, tenants who buy. High fit.
- **relo_agent** — Residential agents in OTHER metros (team leads especially). Agent-to-agent
  referrals with a referral fee are standard practice; they need a reliable Los Angeles County partner.
  High fit.
- **hr_relocation** — HR / people-ops managing relocations at Los Angeles County employers.
  Medium fit — slower, but one relationship = recurring moves.

## Strong-fit signals (raise the score)

- Local to Los Angeles County (office, license, or content about the market) — except relo_agent,
  where OUT of market is the point
- Client-facing and senior (their name brings the client, not a corporate desk)
- Active on LinkedIn about their local market, homeownership, or client stories
- Independent or small-firm (owners refer freely; big-brand staff often have captive partners)

## Disqualifiers (score → 0)

- Other residential agents IN Los Angeles County — competitors, not partners
- Lead-gen vendors, marketing agencies, coaches selling *to* agents
- Corporate/national roles with no local client contact
- Anyone outside the US
