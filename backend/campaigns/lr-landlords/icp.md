# ICP — Self-managing landlords in Los Angeles County

Who we're targeting: **people who personally own rental units in Los Angeles County and
manage them themselves** (no professional property manager between them and the tenant).
They are the ones who must get rent-increase caps, timing, and notices right with no
compliance department behind them — LawfulRent's core buyer. Fit = "does this person
own LA rentals AND handle them personally?"

## Segments (score the fit, label which one)

- **small_landlord** — Owns roughly 1–20 units in LA County (duplex to a couple of small
  buildings), self-managing. Often has a day job or runs it as the day job. Highest fit.
- **investor_operator** — Multifamily investor actively buying/operating LA County
  rentals who still runs their own book (no third-party PM named). High fit.
- **accidental_landlord** — Owns one rental (inherited, moved out and kept it, ADU).
  Profile shows another profession + rental ownership on the side. Medium-high fit —
  they know the least about the rules and feel the most risk.

## Strong-fit signals (raise the score)

- Units in the hard jurisdictions: City of LA (RSO), unincorporated county, Santa Monica,
  West Hollywood, Pasadena, Inglewood, Culver City, Beverly Hills
- Language like "landlord", "owner-operator", "we manage our own units", posts about
  tenants/rents/AB 1482/rent control
- Small-portfolio scale markers (unit counts, "family-owned buildings")
- LA-local (lives here or profile clearly anchored here)

## Disqualifiers (score → 0)

- Institutional scale or staff: REIT/large-operator executives, 100+ unit portfolios with
  a professional team — they have compliance departments
- Works AT a property-management company (that's the lr-property-managers campaign)
- Agents/brokers/leasing professionals with no owned rentals (lr-leasing-agents campaign)
- Wholesalers, flippers, syndication promoters, note investors — no ongoing tenancies
- Vendors selling TO landlords (software, insurance, lending, coaching)
- Properties clearly outside LA County; anyone outside the US
