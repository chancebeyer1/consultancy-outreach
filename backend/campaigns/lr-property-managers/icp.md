# ICP — Property-management people in Los Angeles County

Who we're targeting: **people at residential property-management companies in LA County
who answer rent-increase questions for owner clients** — principals and owners of
independent PM shops, directors/portfolio managers, and senior working PMs. They carry
the "what can we raise on this unit?" question across many owners and many cities —
LawfulRent makes their answer verified and defensible. Fit = "does this person manage
LA residential rentals for third-party owners, at a firm small enough to feel the
compliance load personally?"

## Segments (score the fit, label which one)

- **pm_principal** — Owner/broker/principal of an independent PM firm (roughly
  50–1,000 doors). They carry the liability and set the tools. Highest fit.
- **pm_director** — Director of property management / regional or portfolio manager
  at a small-to-mid firm. Runs the team that fields owner questions daily. High fit.
- **working_pm** — Senior property manager handling their own book of LA buildings
  and owner relationships. Medium-high fit — user and internal champion, not buyer.

## Strong-fit signals (raise the score)

- Residential portfolios spanning multiple LA jurisdictions (City RSO + county +
  Santa Monica/WeHo/Pasadena etc. — the patchwork is their daily problem)
- Independent/local firm branding (not a national institutional operator)
- Third-party management for small private owners (mom-and-pop client base)
- Content about LA rent rules, AB 1482, RSO, owner education

## Disqualifiers (score → 0)

- Institutional operators (Greystar/Lincoln/Equity-scale) and REIT staff — in-house
  legal and enterprise software; not our buyer
- Commercial/industrial/retail-only management; HOA-only managers (no rent increases)
- On-site leasing staff at a single large community (single owner, corporate stack)
- Self-managing landlords with no third-party clients (lr-landlords campaign)
- Vendors selling TO property managers (software, marketing, insurance)
- Outside LA County; outside the US
