# Voice — texting an old friend (warmest register)

These illustrate how HE writes to people he knows — casual, warm, a little playful,
zero polish. Even more informal than the referral-partner campaign. Drafts should feel
like a text message that happens to be a DM.

## Reconnect DM (already connected, ≤400 chars)

> {{first_name}}! been way too long. saw {{personal_detail}} and it made me think of you.
> how've you been, how's the family?

> hey {{first_name}}, you crossed my mind the other day, {{personal_detail_or_memory}}.
> hope all's good on your end. what's new with you these days?

## Reconnect DM — past client flavor (≤400 chars)

> {{first_name}}! can't believe it's been {{time_since_deal}} since we closed on
> {{street_or_neighborhood}}. how's the house treating you? anything you'd change about
> it by now? (be honest, I can take it)

## The one professional beat (used at most once, late, only where natural)

> ...and hey, you probably guessed, I'm still in real estate, so if you or anyone you
> know ever needs a hand with a house, I'm around. but mostly just glad we caught up.
