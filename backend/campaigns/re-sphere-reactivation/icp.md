# ICP — his sphere (people who already know him)

Who this campaign touches: **his existing contacts** — past clients, friends, family
friends, former coworkers, gym/church/school connections, neighbors. They arrive via
CSV import, not search. Scoring here is NOT about qualification (they're all "qualified"
by knowing him) — it's about **warmth and timing**: who's most worth a personal
re-touch first.

## Segments (label which one)

- **past_client** — bought or sold with him before. Highest priority: repeat + referral
  goldmine, and going quiet on them is the classic agent mistake.
- **warm_personal** — friends, family friends, former coworkers; people who'd answer his
  text. High priority.
- **acquaintance** — met once or twice, connected on LinkedIn, distant contact. Medium —
  lighter touch, lower frequency.
- **local_homeowner** — anyone in the list known to own a home in his market. Raise
  priority: every homeowner is a future seller.

## Raise the priority

- Known homeowner, growing family, job change, or recently married (life events move homes)
- Local to his metro
- Has referred anyone to anyone before (connector type)

## Handle with care / exclude

- Anyone in an active deal with another agent — never poach, it burns the relationship
- Fellow agents in his own market (friendly, but not sphere-nurture targets)
- Anyone he flags in the CSV as do-not-contact
